## DEX Platform Requirements & Fees

### PancakeSwap (BSC)
**Fees:**
- No direct listing fee ($0)
- Liquidity provision required (typically 5-20 BNB or equivalent worth of tokens)
- Network gas fees for contract deployment and verification

**Requirements:**
1. Verified BEP-20 smart contract on BscScan
2. Sufficient liquidity pool (recommended minimum $10,000-$50,000)
3. Basic project information (website, social media, whitepaper)
4. Community presence

### Uniswap (Ethereum)
**Fees:**
- No direct listing fee ($0)
- Liquidity provision required (typically 0.1-1 ETH or equivalent worth of tokens)
- Higher gas fees on Ethereum network (can be $50-$500+ depending on network congestion)

**Requirements:**
1. Verified ERC-20 smart contract on Etherscan
2. Liquidity pool creation
3. Token must be tradable on the platform
4. Meet volume/liquidity requirements for tracking sites like CoinGecko

### Raydium (Solana)
**Fees:**
- No direct listing fee ($0)
- Liquidity provision required (typically 0.5-2 SOL or equivalent worth of tokens)
- Low network fees (~$0.01-$0.10 per transaction)

**Requirements:**
1. SPL token on Solana blockchain
2. Liquidity pool creation
3. At least 0.9 SOL in wallet to cover fees
4. Basic project information

## CEX Platform Requirements & Fees

### MEXC
**Fees:**
- Listing fees typically range from $0-$50,000+
- Some reports indicate fees around $20,000-$120,000
- Additional marketing and security deposits may be required

**Requirements:**
1. Completed application form with project details
2. Project whitepaper
3. Legal compliance documentation
4. Active social media presence
5. Audited smart contracts
6. Tokenomics and distribution details

### Gate.io
**Fees:**
- Officially states no listing fee ($0)
- However, competition for listings means projects often need to spend on marketing
- Indirect costs can range from $5,000-$100,000+

**Requirements:**
1. Application through official channel
2. Project documentation (whitepaper, roadmap)
3. Team information and project legitimacy
4. Smart contract audit
5. Active community presence
6. Legal compliance
