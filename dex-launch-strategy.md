## How to Get Your Project Listed

### 1. **Permissionless DEXs (Most Common Approach)**
These platforms allow anyone to create a trading pair without approval:

**Steps:**
1. Deploy your token smart contract on the appropriate blockchain
2. Obtain tokens and equivalent value in the paired asset (e.g., USDT/ETH/BNB)
3. Navigate to the DEX's "Create Pool" or "Add Liquidity" section
4. Specify the token pair and amounts you want to contribute
5. Confirm the transaction through your wallet
6. Your token will be immediately tradable

**Advantages:**
- No approval process
- No listing fees
- Immediate availability
- Full control over pricing

**Disadvantages:**
- No official promotion from the DEX
- Responsibility for liquidity provision
- Risk of low trading volume

### 2. **Curated DEXs (Less Common)**
Some platforms have a review process:

**Steps:**
1. Submit a listing application through their official process
2. Pay any required fees (if applicable)
3. Wait for review and approval
4. Follow their specific instructions for pool creation
5. Provide required documentation and information

**Examples:**
- 1inch (used to have a listing process, now mostly permissionless)
- Some enterprise-focused DEXs

## Detailed Listing Process for Permissionless DEXs

### Step 1: Prepare Your Token
- Ensure your token follows the correct standard (ERC-20 for Ethereum, BEP-20 for BSC, etc.)

### Step 2: Choose Your Platform
- Consider factors like:
  - Transaction fees (Ethereum has high fees, BSC and Polygon are cheaper)
  - Target audience
  - Existing community
  - Integration with other DeFi protocols

### Step 3: Prepare Liquidity
- Decide on your initial liquidity amount (remember we adjusted this to $100K in your file)
- Ensure you have an equal value of your token and the paired asset
- Consider using a small portion of your total supply initially

### Step 4: Create the Pool
1. Connect your wallet to the DEX
2. Navigate to the "Pool" or "Liquidity" section
3. Select "Create Pool" or "Add Liquidity"
4. Enter your token contract address
5. Specify the amounts of both tokens you want to add
6. Confirm the transaction

- Add token information to blockchain explorers
- Create a token logo and description
- Set up price charts through third-party services
